Welcome to my writeup for the 2025 SANS Holiday Hack Challenge!! I had a lot of fun working through this year's challenges, and I learned a lot! I hope that this writeup helps you out as you work through the challenges on your own. 

I've broken this down into a section with all of the challenges and then a section for all of the custom scripts that I wrote. I've tried to include screenshots of each challenge to highlight various points of interest and I've tried to include comments in my scripts to help understand what is going on.
